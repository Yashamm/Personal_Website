Microstructure Process Map for Laser Directed Energy Deposition of Al-0.5Sc-0.5Si alloy


Mentors - Dr. Amit Arora (IITGn, AMPRG) & Dr. Amit Kumar Singh (IITB)

Objective: Determining microstructure morphology is critical in additive manufacturing as the final properties of the sample are directly correlated with the microstructure. Thus, the ability to control the microstructure is vital. Therefore, the variation of microstructure morphology during Laser Directed Energy Deposition of Al-0.5Sc-0.5Si alloy for varying parameters is studied.
