Pixy Stick Drop Challenge: Inspired from MARS Smaple Return Mission


Course Instructor - Dr. Tim Simpson (PSU)

Objective: To come up with a additively manufactured container design that can hold 30 pixy stick and should be able to withstnad a safe impact when dropeed from 100ft! Few important contraints were - the total weight should be less than 0.5 Kg, use of Laser Powder Bed Fusion (LPBF) and fixed build volume.
