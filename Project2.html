Heat Transfer and Fluid Flow model for Multilayer Multimaterial Laser-Directed Energy Deposition (LDED)


Mentors - Dr. Amit Arora (IITGn, AMPRG) & Dr. Amit Kumar Singh (IITB)

Objective: The requirement of functionally graded, multimaterial material deposition is rising due to the possibility of building parts with enhanced and unique properties as compared to traditional manufacturing techniques. Therefore, in the project heat transfer and fluid flow model for multimaterial LDED was developed to study the thermal cycle and melt pool dynamics during multimaterial multilayer laser-directed energy deposition (LDED)

Technical Details and Results:

Heat transfer and fluid flow FORTRAN code of spot welding is transformed for laser-directed energy deposition. Being a numerical model, changes in the mesh after each layer, dwell time, laser movement, remelting, etc. phenomenon were changed in the algorithm. Thermal cycles and melt pool dynamics were analyzed for the resulting deposition simulation.
